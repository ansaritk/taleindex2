Overview

Tale Index is a powerful platform that synchronizes every stage of your marketing campaign. From concept to final delivery, keep your team aligned and make sure your ads connect with audiences on an emotional level.

Brief Analysis & Information Validation

Start with clarity. Upload client briefs and align on goals instantly. Our AI-powered platform analyzes briefs and builds up a summary for consistency, ensuring everyone is on the same page from the start.

Concept with Budget Approval

Tale Index keeps everyone in sync through the creative process. Share concepts, gather feedback, and get approvals—our collaboration tools streamline the workflow and keep everything moving.

Script Analysis and Approval

Make sure your message resonates. Our proprietary algorithm analyzes sensitivity and key metrics such as emotions, engagement, brand positioning, helping you fine-tune ads to connect with your audience’s emotions and make a lasting impact.

Storyboarding and Final Production

From creating storyboards to audiovisual production, ensure each stage reflects the core message. Tale Index brings your team together to create cohesive, compelling content that aligns with campaign goals.

Budget Approval & Optimization

Easy to track the Production Budget, categorising it into Materials, Location & People for easy reference, follow up, and approval.

Final Delivery with Neuromarketing Insights

Make sure your message resonates. Our neuromarketing technology analyzes emotional responses, helping you fine-tune ads to connect with your audience’s emotions and make a lasting impact.
